Popular Tourist Spots

Plan ahead as here are some of the popular areas with unique landscapes and historical backgrounds visited by tourists in Cebu!

Magellan's Cross

Magellan's Cross Pavilion is a stone kiosk in Cebu City, Philippines. The structure is situated on Plaza Sugbo beside the Basilica del Santo Niño It houses a Christian cross that was planted by explorers

Learn More

Bantayan Island

Bantayan Island is an off-the-beaten-path tourist destination in Cebu and is said to be the Boracay of the south! You can go to white sand beaches, saltwater lagoons, mangrove boardwalks, cave pools, old ruins, skydiving, and island hopping, to name just a few.

Learn More

Cebu Taoist Temple

Cebu Taoist Temple is a Taoist temple located in Beverly Hills Subdivision of Cebu City, Philippines. The temple is built by Cebu's substantial Filipino-Chinese community in 1972.

Carbon Food Market

Carbon Market is an open-air night market offering a wide selection of locally produced goods, right in the heart of Cebu City. Vendors sell their products, including Cebu delicacies, Sutukil food, and a variety of food stalls.
